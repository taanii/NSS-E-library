Project Mission

An initiative by NSS Unit of Datta Meghe College of Engineering to create a 24×7 digital platform for the students from Class 1st to 10th with all types of materials like e-text books, story books, poems, short stories, picture books and audio books.

An initiative to be one of a kind and to keep up the spirit of learning.

Story Books

There is no friend as loyal as a book.

Access various fascinating story books which teaches you many valuable lessons and helps you to develop your imagination.

Short Stories

Let's add up the short learning in a better way!

We have brought you all the tales in your preferred language. We provide comedy, action, detective and and moral stories here.

Picture Books

Books are a uniquely portable magic.

Pictures do say a lot than some mere words. Get access to some amazing picture books for young readers and help inspire visual thinking.

Audio Books

Find a listen for every moment.

Why not experience the same stories and poems in a different way? 'Audio Section' is the answer. Get access to audio books of stories and poems in Marathi anytime!
